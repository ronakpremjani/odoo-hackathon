TransitOps. Fleet operations, finally under control.

Every day, transport companies lose hours to spreadsheets and phone calls. The same vehicle gets booked twice. A suspended driver ends up on a live route. Maintenance slips through the cracks. Fuel costs pile up with no clear picture of return on investment.

TransitOps is a transport operations ERP built to replace that fragmentation. One platform for fleet, drivers, dispatches, maintenance, fuel, and financial oversight.

Sign in with secure JWT authentication. Each user sees only what their role allows.

Welcome to TransitOps Core, your fleet operations and ROI intelligence control center. Four KPI cards update in real time from server-side aggregations. Fleet utilization. Operational cost. Estimated revenue at two dollars fifty per mile. Return on investment.

Register vehicles, manage operators with license validation and safety scores, dispatch trips with automatic status transitions, schedule maintenance, log fuel and expenses, and review live analytics.

When someone breaks the rules, the system stops them. Vehicle already on trip. Driver suspended. License expired. Cargo over capacity. Vehicle in maintenance. Clear errors. No silent failures.

Built with React, Express, MongoDB, and role-based access control. Clean architecture with services, repositories, and enforced business rules.

TransitOps replaces spreadsheet chaos with enforced workflows and live financial intelligence. One platform. One source of truth. Built for teams that move the world. Thank you, Odoo Hackathon judges.
